# Solar Background Lock

The cinematic solar-system background is a protected project surface.

- Do not use images from `frontend/planets/` to replace orbiting planets in the home-page canvas.
- Do not redraw, override, or simplify the sun, orbit paths, lighting, depth, or planet rendering in `frontend/js/app.js`.
- Provided planet images are allowed only in UI: navigation icons, circular detail-page hero images, information panels, and planet-to-planet footer links.
- Changes to emotional detail pages must remain isolated in `frontend/css/emotion-pages.css` and `frontend/js/emotion-pages.js` whenever possible.
